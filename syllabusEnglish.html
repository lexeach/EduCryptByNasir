Why EduCrypt ?

Hello friend,

It was 2014, a colleague of mine came to meet me and he had a friend with him, both of them advised me to buy Bitcoin, then its price was only $500. But I refused. At that time, I did not see any special value in it and considered Bitcoin to be an international fraud, which was being run by some powerful tech lobby. My doubt was further increased by the negative news spreading in the market. Due to these negative perceptions, I rejected it in the early days, thinking it was very risky, and I doubted its future. I asked that colleague what is Bitcoin and how it works but he could not explain its technical explanation to me. I thought it was just a fraud, and I flatly refused to invest.

Time passed and four years later in 2017 I got a call from an acquaintance and he told me that the price of Bitcoin has reached $15,000 and advised me to buy Bitcoin. I was shocked. The rapid price increase left me stunned and curious as to how something I had rejected a few years ago could grow so fast. I asked my old question to that acquaintance as well, what is Bitcoin and how does it work, but he too could not answer me, his entire focus was on investing money. This sudden surge further piqued my interest, and I finally decided to understand Bitcoin in depth. This was the moment when I entered the world of blockchain and cryptocurrency. Today when I look back, I realize how much I had underestimated the potential of Bitcoin. When I delved deeper into this technology and understood the basic principles of blockchain, I realized how wrong my thoughts were. Now, I understand the amazing potential of cryptocurrency, and I regret that misconceptions in the early days influenced my decision. This missed opportunity taught me how much it is necessary to learn and research about new technologies, rather than making preconceived notions about them.

As an investor, I ignored Bitcoin when it was worth only $500, which is a hard lesson I will never forget. Back then, I dismissed it because I was not aware of blockchain technology. I did not fully understand its potential, and it cost me dearly. If only I had learned more about that emerging product, I would have had bigger advantages today. Now, I make it a priority to stay informed about new technologies, because I know how important it is to spot opportunities.

After not being able to buy Bitcoin in the early days, I decided to understand blockchain and cryptocurrencies in depth through my own efforts. The more I learned, the more I was convinced that this technology is the future. Now, I want no one else to miss opportunities due to such misunderstandings.

That is why we have started the EduCrypt online tutorial series, dedicated to spreading knowledge about blockchain and cryptocurrencies. Through this platform, we want to provide people with the necessary understanding about this changing digital world and we have tried to make those who are not technically proficient also get the basic knowledge about this future-changing technology in simple words, then we will consider our efforts successful.

Your friend,
Gautam
